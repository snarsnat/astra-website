# Astra Website

A website for Astra that deliberately avoids all modern web design tropes.

## What This Website Avoids

This website was created with specific instructions to avoid every common trope in modern web design:

### 1. **No Hero Section Formula**
- No massive centered headline in oversized bold sans-serif
- No subheadline with words like "seamless," "effortless," "transform"
- No gradient text or glowing effects
- No big central 3D mockup or abstract tech orb

### 2. **No Default Color & Aesthetic Tropes**
- No dark mode with electric purple/teal/cyan accents
- No glassmorphism (frosted/blurred cards)
- No subtle grain or noise texture
- No neon glows on buttons or borders
- No over-saturated gradients

### 3. **No Layout & Grid Tropes**
- No perfectly centered symmetrical single-column layout
- No bento-style grid
- No excessive whitespace without purpose
- No section dividers with wavy lines or particle fields
- No 3-column feature grids with icon + title + one-sentence description
- No testimonial carousels with AI-generated headshots

### 4. **No Imagery & Visual Signatures**
- No uncanny-valley people with perfect symmetry
- No 3D product mockups with impossible reflections
- No abstract "data visualization" blobs
- No hands with wrong number of fingers
- No "diverse team" photos from the same AI prompt

### 5. **No Typography & Micro-Interaction Tropes**
- Not limited to 2-3 font weights
- No kinetic/animated typography on scroll
- No micro-animations on every interactive element
- No custom cursors with trailing dots
- No magnetic hover effects

### 6. **No Structural & Content Tropes**
- No overlong vertical scroll with 10-15 identical sections
- No pricing tables with three tiers and "Most Popular" badge
- No fake "AI personalization" demo
- No instant chatbot popup
- No "Why Us" lists phrased as profound insights

### 7. **No Copywriting Patterns**
- Avoids words: delve, tapestry, realm, embark, harness, pivotal, seamless, vibrant, intricate
- No "Rule of three: Faster. Smarter. Better."
- No opening lines like "In today's fast-paced digital landscape..."
- No describing every feature as "intelligent," "AI-powered," or "cutting-edge"

### 8. **No Anti-AI/Rebellion Tropes**
- No deliberate messiness or asymmetric layouts
- No hand-scribbled annotations
- No brutalist or "technical mono" style
- No maximalist collage layering
- No visible human touches as rebellion

## What This Website Actually Has

1. **Honest content** - Says what the product does without buzzwords
2. **Simple design** - Clean, readable, functional
3. **Human scale** - Text sizes that are actually readable
4. **Useful information** - No filler content
5. **Basic interactivity** - Just what's needed, no more

## How to View

Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
